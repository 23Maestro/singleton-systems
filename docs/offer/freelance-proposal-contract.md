# Freelance Proposal Contract

## Read First

Use [confirmed-copy.md](confirmed-copy.md) for current offer language. A proposal
answers the client's decision; it does not restate the post or perform agency
authority.

## Shape

```text
one direct question or observation
one sentence showing the real decision understood
one or two truthful proof anchors
one clear next step
```

Use plain language and concrete nouns. Do not invent results, client scale,
metrics, or niche experience. When the fit is adjacent, say so briefly and
bridge through the real pattern: narrative judgment, asset organization,
repeatable review, or reliable delivery.

## Output Branches

- Cover letter only: two tight paragraphs plus compact links.
- Supporting questions: answer them separately; do not repeat them in the
  letter.
- Short post: keep the proposal shorter than the post.
- Platform research: record platform, question, date, why it matters, and next
  check. Do not treat an unverified platform as a qualified lead.

## Proof

Use only relevant evidence: course production, Prospect ID video/workflow work,
broadcast media, long-running freelance delivery, or workflow cleanup. Include
the portfolio or a targeted sample only when it advances the decision.

The simple AI loop

Use this every time.

1
Start small Pick one tiny task with no sensitive information.
2
Give context Tell AI the goal, audience, tone, and format.
3
Correct it Say what is wrong: too long, too cold, too vague, too salesy.
4
Save what works Keep your best prompts in Notes, Notion, or a simple doc.

Privacy rule

Easy rule. No exceptions.

✓
Use placeholders Customer A, Account A, Vehicle A, Appointment A.
×
Do not paste private details No names, phones, addresses, account numbers, VINs, payment info, or internal-only records.
!
Verify before using AI can sound confident and still be wrong.

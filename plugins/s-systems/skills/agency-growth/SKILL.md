# Agency Growth

Use this for direct client acquisition, not Upwork/Fiverr proposals. For a
marketplace post, use `freelance-gig-proposals`; for proof-led social content,
use `offer-proof-content`.

## Contract

1. Identify the offer, ICP, trigger signal, and desired next action.
2. Use a real signal or proof point; do not invent personalization.
3. Choose the smallest useful output: account brief, first email, follow-up,
   objection response, sequence, or pipeline next action.
4. Keep one clear CTA and one requested action.
5. Run the Final Human Pass before presenting copy as final.

## Strategy Mode

Use for ICP, account tiering, offer angle, and outreach sequence decisions.

```text
ICP:
Signal:
Problem hypothesis:
Proof:
Channel:
Next action:
```

## Execution Mode

Use for a concise email, DM, follow-up, objection response, or post-call note.

- Lead with the observed signal or relevant proof.
- Connect it to one likely workflow problem.
- Ask a small, specific question or make one low-friction CTA.
- Do not use fabricated metrics, generic praise, or multi-CTA messages.

## Boundaries

- Do not claim a prospect has a problem without evidence.
- Do not automate sends.
- Do not replace Opportunity HQ as durable pipeline/task truth.
- Do not load legacy Agency agent files; their generic playbooks are preserved
  outside this active skill to avoid unrelated context.
